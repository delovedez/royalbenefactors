Our Story

- As a 17 year young lady, I have always had a heart for the needs of children.
Growing up as part of my dreams, I wanted to have a foundation one day but didn’t know where to start from. I took this opportunity to talk to my parents on the impact I wanted to make in the society, I knew if they had they’d have supported me with everything I needed.
I thought of this, even though I have parents yet things are difficult sometimes, what about those people who have no parents, I have a home but where do the homeless sleep when it rains, how do they get to feed day in day out.
This hit me so hard, I then discussed this with my closest friends and they decided to help me out after that, a whole lot of people joined since we all wanted to change the world. Through how united we were, we made the foundation or association official.
Royal Benefactors (Helping the needy as we can).

Aims:

  • Create jobs opportunities for the needy
  • Bring the people on the street back to their homes
